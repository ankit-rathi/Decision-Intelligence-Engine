### Chapter 20 — The Data-Driven Organization

---

### **Learning loops create compounding advantage**

---

### Core Concept

[Icon Suggestion: brain]

* Decision learning — treat decisions as inputs
* Outcome signals — capture real-world results
* Feedback loops — connect outcomes to learning
* Continuous improvement — refine decisions over time

---

### Learning Loop

[Icon Suggestion: cycle]

* Decision input — trigger actions in system
* Outcome generation — observe real-world effects
* Learning process — analyze outcomes for insight
* Decision refinement — improve future decision logic

---

### System Components

[Icon Suggestion: layers]

* Data capture — record decision outcomes
* Intelligence layer — generate insights and patterns
* Decision logic — update rules and models
* Execution systems — apply decisions operationally

---

### Enabling Mechanisms

[Icon Suggestion: gears]

* Experimentation systems — test alternative decisions
* Data literacy — enable interpretation across teams
* Governance controls — ensure reliable decisions
* Incentive alignment — reward learning behaviors

---

### Failure Modes

[Icon Suggestion: warning]

* Insight isolation — learning trapped in silos
* Intuition bias — decisions ignore data signals
* Static processes — no feedback integration
* Misaligned incentives — reward short-term outputs

---

### Strategic Outcome

[Icon Suggestion: compass]

* Compounding learning — decisions improve cumulatively
* Adaptive systems — respond to changing conditions
* Embedded intelligence — integrate into workflows
* Decision enterprise — scale learning across organization
